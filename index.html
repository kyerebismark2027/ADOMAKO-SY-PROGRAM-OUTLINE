Help & Guide

🔐 How to Login

  1. Enter the username: adomako
  2. Enter the password: cop2028
  3. Click the "Login" button
  4. If credentials are correct, you'll be redirected to the dashboard

📝 How to Enter Program Data

  1. Navigate to the Input page (Dashboard)
  2. Find the day you want to program (Monday through Sunday)
  3. Fill in the following fields for each day:
    • Opening Prayer
    • Worship
    • Sermon
    • Intensive Prayer
    • Offering
    • Conductor
  4. Data is automatically saved to your browser

📊 How to Generate Summary

  1. Enter all your program data
  2. Click the "Generate Summary" button
  3. View your complete program in a professional table format

📥 How to Download as Image

  1. Generate the summary view
  2. Click the "Download as Image" button
  3. Wait for the download to complete
  4. The summary will be saved as a PNG image

📱 How to Share on WhatsApp

  1. Generate the summary view
  2. Click the "Share on WhatsApp" button
  3. WhatsApp will open with your program as a formatted message
  4. Select a contact or group to share with
💡 Tip: Your data is automatically saved in your browser's local storage. This means your program data will persist even after you close the browser or refresh the page!
